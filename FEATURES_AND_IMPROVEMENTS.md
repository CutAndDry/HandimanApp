# Handiman App - Features & Improvements Roadmap

**Last Updated:** January 13, 2026  
**Project:** HandimanApp - Field Service Management Software  
**Competitive Analysis:** Based on Jobber, Housecall Pro, and ServiceTitan

---

## Executive Summary

Based on competitive analysis of industry leaders (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan), this document outlines features and improvements to make HandimanApp a competitive field service management platform. The competition shows that success hinges on:
- **Revenue Growth**: 35-44% average revenue growth in first year
- **Time Savings**: 8-12 hours saved per week
- **End-to-End Workflows**: From lead generation to invoice/payment
- **Mobile-First Design**: Field teams need powerful mobile apps
- **AI/Automation**: Intelligent pricing, scheduling, and communication

---

## Phase 1: Core Workflow Enhancements (Next 4-6 weeks)

### 1.1 Lead Management & Job Intake
**What Competitors Do:**
- Jobber: AI receptionist answers calls, books jobs 24/7
- Housecall Pro: Automated marketing campaigns, online booking
- ServiceTitan: Call booking software, lead pipeline

**Handiman Improvements:**
- [ ] **Online Booking System** - Allow customers to self-book appointments
  - Calendar view showing available slots
  - Automated confirmation emails/SMS
  - Integration with Google Calendar
  
- [ ] **Lead Pipeline Dashboard** - Track leads from inquiry to job
  - Kanban board for job stages (Lead → Quote → Scheduled → In Progress → Complete → Invoice → Paid)
  - Lead scoring and prioritization
  - Automated follow-up reminders
  
- [ ] **Customer Portal** - Self-service for customers
  - View job history
  - Track current job status
  - Access invoices and make payments
  - Request additional services

### 1.2 Advanced Scheduling & Dispatching
**What Competitors Do:**
- Real-time GPS tracking of field teams
- Drag-and-drop calendar scheduling
- Route optimization to save travel time
- Automatic job assignment based on tech skills/availability

**Handiman Improvements:**
- [ ] **Map View for Jobs** - Visual dispatch board
  - See all jobs on a map
  - Estimate travel time between jobs
  - Quick reassignment by dragging pins
  
- [ ] **Smart Scheduling** - AI-powered scheduling
  - Suggest optimal job sequence for technicians
  - Consider travel time, skill requirements, customer preferences
  - Automatic conflict detection
  
- [ ] **Technician Skills & Certifications** - Track what each tech can do
  - Assign jobs based on required skills
  - Plan training needs
  - Track licenses/certifications
